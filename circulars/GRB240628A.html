T. Preis, B. Biltzinger, J. Burgess & J. Greiner (all MPE Garching) report:

The public trigdat data of the Fermi Gamma-ray Burst Monitor (GBM) trigger
741288821 at 17:33:36 on 28 June 2024 were automatically fitted for spectrum
and sky location with BALROG (Burgess et al. 2018, MNRAS 476, 1427;
Berlato et al. 2019, ApJ 873, 60).

The best-fit position is:
RA(2000.0) = 331.0 deg
Decl.(2000.0) = -38.0 deg
The 1 sigma statistical error radius is 4.0 deg.
We estimate an additional systematic error of 1 deg.
