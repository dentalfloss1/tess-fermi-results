MASTER-SAAO robotic telescope (Global MASTER-Net: http://observ.pereplet.ru, Lipunov et al., 2010, Advances in Astronomy, vol. 2010, 30L) located in South Africa (South African Astronomical Observatory) was pointed to the  GRB210702.80 17 sec after notice time and 822 sec after trigger time at 2021-07-02 19:20:56 UT. On our first (160s exposure)  set we  found 1 optical transient within Swift error-box (ra=168.567 dec=-36.7408 r=0.05) brighter than  18.1.
