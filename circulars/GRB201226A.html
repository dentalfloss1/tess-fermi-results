report on behalf of the Insight-HXMT team:

At 2020-12-26T13:16:26.60 (T0), Insight-HXMT/HE detected 
GRB 201226A (trigger ID: HEB201226553) in a routine search of the data, 
which also triggered Fermi/GBM (trigger 630681391).

The Insight-HXMT/HE light curve mainly consists of multiple 
pulses with a duration (T90) of 69.119 s measured from T0+0.34 s. 
The 1-ms peak rate, measured from T0+69.194 s, is 706 cnts/sec. 
The total counts from this burst is 10857 counts. 
URL_LC: http://twiki.ihep.ac.cn/pub/HXMT/GRBList/HEB201226553_lc.jpg 

All measurements above are made with the CsI detectors operating in the 
regular mode with the energy range of about 80-800 keV (deposited energy). 
Only gamma-rays with energy greater than about 200 keV can penetrate 
the spacecraft and leave signals in the CsI detectors installed inside 
of the telescope. 
 
Insight-HXMT is the first Chinese space X-ray telescope, which was 
funded jointly by the China National Space Administration (CNSA) and 
the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). 
More information about it could be found at: 
http://www.hxmt.org.
