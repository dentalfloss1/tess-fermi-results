MASTER-Tavrida robotic telescope (Global MASTER-Net: http://observ.pereplet.ru, Lipunov et al., 2010, Advances in Astronomy, vol. 2010, 30L) located in Russia (Lomonosov MSU, SAI Crimea astronomical station) was pointed to the  GRB191020.81 20 sec after notice time and 297 sec after trigger time at 2019-10-20 19:25:00 UT. On our 6-th (180s exposure)  set , obtained 925 sec after tigger time at 2019-10-20 19:35:29 UT, we  found 1 optical transient within Swift error-box (ra=339.992 dec=-17.3211 r=0.1) brighter than 18.55.


 T-Tmid      Date      Time       Expt.        Ra                Dec           Mag
---------|---------------------|-------|-----------------|-----------------|-------
    1015   2019-10-20 19:35:29     180   (22h 39m 57.886s , -17d 24m 35.54s)    15.97

The OT has irregular early time  behaviour since 1139 till 1532.5 seconds after the trigger.
The 5-sigma upper limit has been about 18.55mag
The message may be cited.
